My artistic practice exists between land art, environmental art and site-specific installation.

I work with landscape as both material and collaborator, creating installations that reveal invisible relations between people, memory and place.

Each project grows from a particular site. Instead of imposing form, I allow materials, weather, history and local context to shape the work.
