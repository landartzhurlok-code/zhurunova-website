CV

Marharyta Zhurunova is a Ukrainian artist working between printmaking, installation and expanded graphic practices. Her work explores the sacred, the non-human, memory, emotional trauma and the relationship between people and landscape.

Born on 29 November 1994 in Vinnytsia, Ukraine.

Since 2015 she has collaborated with Bohdan Lokatyr on environmental and site-specific art projects under the name Zhurunova & Lokatyr.
